Artist, musician and workshop facilitator


Based in London, UK





I am interested in reimagining the world, and do this by unpicking our understandings of power and their legacies, aiming to facilitate a nuanced dialogue around the colonial matrix we live within. My practice tries to find ways of de-linking from coloniality that I can embody and realise in relation to my context as an Armenian-Iranian person born and living in the West, perceived as white/assimilated or non-white in different situations, and gendered as a woman. Through performance, music and other media, I intend to produce new knowledge in forms that have long been dismissed.
